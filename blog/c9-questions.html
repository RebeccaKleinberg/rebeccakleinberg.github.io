There is No Such Thing as a Dumb Question

But Certainly There is a Dumb Way to Write a Question

Questions should not be asked until you have researced and not been able to find the answer elsewhere. Good questions should be titled with a specific refernece to what the question is about.It's best to give examples and show what you have tried rather then ask a general question. Questions should be straight-forward and to the point, rather than superfluous.
